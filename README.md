# The Ripple Universe Theory

**A Hypothesis on Gravitationally-Regulated Quantum Fluctuation Waves and Dark Matter Genesis**

This research paper proposes an alternative cosmological model in which the universe exhibits a wave-like nature driven by gravitationally-regulated quantum fluctuations. These spacetime ripples are hypothesized to explain the apparent cosmic expansion and the genesis of dark matter and dark energy as natural by-products of the universal wave dynamics, challenging the traditional Big Bang framework.

## Abstract
The Ripple Universe Theory offers a unified explanation for several longstanding cosmological mysteries: the source of cosmic expansion, the origin and behavior of dark matter, and the gravitational fingerprint that defines them both. The model treats the universe as a dynamic wave system rather than an explosively expanding void.
